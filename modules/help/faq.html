How do I play BeamNG.drive?
Please refer to the quickstart guide
How do I change the camera?
Press C to cycle vehicle cameras, or Shift + C to use free camera
How do I change vehicle?
You can use the vehicle selector from the dashboard using ESC or press CTRL + E.
How do I spawn more vehicles?
You can use the vehicle selector from the dashboard using ESC or press CTRL + E.
How do I open the map editor?
The map editor can be accessed by pressing F11 when running in windowed mode.
Where can I find mods?
You can download mods from the BeamNG.drive forum showroom
Where should I install mods?
Download the .zip of the vehicle or level you wish to install. Drop the zip in: 
My Documents\BeamNG.drive\mods
For more information and futher instuctions please visit our wiki here.
How do I use mods?
After installing a mod, restart BeamNg.drive and find the mod in the vehicle or level selection menu.
How do I update the game?
Steam automatically installs updates when you are connected to the internet.
